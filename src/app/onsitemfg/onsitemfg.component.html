Mobile Duct Machines—Fast, On-Site Production of Round Spiral Duct


McGill AirFlow can save you money with our Uni-Duct duct system design service. We use our state-of-the-art Uni-Duct software to perform time-consuming design work for you. It designs duct systems that have the lowest possible material and operating costs. Uni-Duct provides the most efficiently balanced and sized duct system designs available.

Each Mobile Duct Machine has been designed to meet the needs of the installation contractor. It quickly fabricates varying lengths of spiral duct in diameters from 3 to 72 inches. The contractor can control the amount and dimensions of the duct as it is fabricated, thereby eliminating waste and the need for storage. Only one person is needed to operate the machine. Other workers can install the finished product immediately, without wasting time unloading the factory-fabricated duct from delivery trucks.

Key Advandages of a Mobile Duct Machine
  • Reduces material handling costs by eliminating the need to unload delivery trucks or stage materials.
  • Reduces freight expenses.
  • Eliminates time spent waiting for materials to be delivered.
  • Eliminates potential for damaged, lost, or late delivered materials.
  • Can be moved to different locations on the jobsite.
  • Improves project scheduling.

Mobile Duct Machines — A photo tour


Each self-contained Mobile Duct Machine is equipped with an air compressor, welding machine, steel decoiler, heating, and lighting. A separate truck-mounted electrical generator is also available.

The Mobile Duct Machines use a pneumatic or hydraulic handling table, which allows the operator to deliver the duct directly into the hands of the contractor.

Each mobile unit is equipped with a 2-ton overhead crane for easy and convenient material handling.

The 45-foot Mobile Duct Machines can be moved easily to indoor work areas.
